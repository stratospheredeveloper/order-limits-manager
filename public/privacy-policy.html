Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

Stratosphere Dev ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Order Limits Manager application (the "App") for Shopify merchants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Store Information

When you install our App, we collect:

  • Your Shopify store domain
  • Store owner email address
  • Product information (titles, IDs, variants) that you choose to apply order limits to

Order Limit Rules

We store the order limit rules you create, including:

  • Product or variant IDs
  • Minimum and maximum quantity limits
  • Custom error messages
  • Rule status (enabled/disabled)

Cart Validation Data

When customers shop on your store, we temporarily process:

  • Cart contents (product IDs and quantities only)
  • Validation results

Note: We do NOT store customer personal information, payment details, or order history.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide the order limits functionality
  • Validate cart contents against your configured rules
  • Display appropriate error messages to customers
  • Maintain and improve the App
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Railway.app infrastructure with industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure database with access controls
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. We only share data:

  • With Shopify, as necessary to provide the App functionality
  • With our hosting provider (Railway.app) for infrastructure purposes
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • You have the App installed on your store
  • Necessary to provide support and comply with legal obligations

When you uninstall the App, your data is deleted within 30 days, except as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information through the App dashboard
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data by uninstalling the App or contacting us
  • Export: Receive your data in a portable format

Cookies and Tracking

Our App does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Cart validation occurs server-side without storing customer identifiers.

Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for Shopify merchants only and does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the App dashboard. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This App complies with:

  • Shopify's App Store Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)
